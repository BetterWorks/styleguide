Dashboard Modules

Dashboard Modules are the heart and soul of the BetterWorks app. They give users an overview of all the import information they're looking for, compacted for easy readability and digestion.


My Summary

The My Summary module is the users BetterWorks business card. This module displays the users profile image, name, title, current goals, checkins, cheers received, and any badges they've earned.

Goal Stats

The Goal Stats module is a breakdown of the users overall goal progress. The large dial on top displays their overall and expected goal progress. Below the dial is a list of all the users' goals in progress. Each item in the list includes the goal name, progress pie, progress percent, and last update. Progress does not include private goals.

Risk Areas

Total Goals

The Total Goals module displays the total number of goals split into three sections: green, yellow, and red. Green represents goals that are on track, yellow represents goals that are 25% behind schedule, and red represents goals that are 50% behind schedule.
