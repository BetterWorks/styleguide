INTRO

Text is critical when communicating with customers. Typography ensures the text is legible and provides visual hierarchy so that information is organized and scannable. Proxima Nova is the standard typeface.

TYPEFACE

Stylistically, Proxima Nova straddles the gap between typefaces like Futura and Akzidenz Grotesk. The result is a hybrid that combinines humanistic proportions with a somewhat geometric appearance.
