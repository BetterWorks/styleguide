MAIN COLOR PALETTE

BetterWorks mobile products are key members of BetterWorks product family. We embrace our overall product design principles and value the difference in mobile context at the same time. We expect to always deliver a killer mobile product that meets high expectation of functionality and experience for our users. When we evaluate our opportunity, problems to be solved and potential solution directions, we consider the following aspects:

Blue is BetterWorks’ primary brand color. It emphasizes important, mainly interactive elements in the product. We use blue in our logo and for primary CTAs. It’s also used in many of our icons.

Red is mainly used to indicate errors. For example, it’s used to represent the progress status of a goal that’s behind and needs attention, an error state, passed due date, etc.

Green is mainly used for progress meters and success states. For example, when a goal is on track, it’s green.

Yellow is mainly used for progress meters. It indicates a warning state, in between success and danger. If a goal is yellow it’s at risk.

Orange is used for updates. We use orange to let the user know to refresh the BetterWorks page and is also our primary color for onboarding.

Light Blue is used for hover or selected state and unread messages. For example, objective row becomes light blue when it’s selected.
