BetterWorks mobile products are key members of BetterWorks product family. We embrace our overall product design principles and value the difference in mobile context at the same time. We expect to always deliver a killer mobile product that meets high expectation of functionality and experience for our users. When we evaluate our opportunity, problems to be solved and potential solution directions, we consider the following aspects:

Serve BetterWorks users even better

Our mobile users are the BetterWorks user-base. We focus on our existing clients and aim to deliver unique values via mobile experience.

Fill a real need

We must address a real need and do a real job. Our judgement is based on: whether people are trying to fill the need right now; whether people are spending money or time trying to fill this need; whether people are finding hacks/workarounds to fill this need.

Mobile-first

We must address a need that can only or best be filled by a native mobile application. if it can be done on desktop just as well, it's not for us.

#1 confidence

We aim to fill needs in a way that we are confident that we have the #1 confidence in the market and have the motiviation to iteratively update it to keep it the best.
