INTRO

BetterWorks product colors are used in BetterWorks web app, mobile web and mobile native apps. BetterWorks color system consists mainly of three collections of colors used for main product design, dashboard design, and avatar image design respectively The light versions of the standard colors below are hover states.

MAIN COLOR PALETTE

BetterWorks mobile products are key members of BetterWorks product family. We embrace our overall product design principles and value the difference in mobile context at the same time. We expect to always deliver a killer mobile product that meets high expectation of functionality and experience for our users.
