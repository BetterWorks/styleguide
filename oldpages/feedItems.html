Feed Items

As an owner of a goal, I want to see a record of all progress, edits, and social activity that has occurred on my page. Connecting goals is like a social contract. Timeline is the vehicle.


Green Circles
Green circles indicate edits.
Blue Circles
Blue circles indicate comments, contributions, and check ins.
Orange Circles
Orange circles indicate social interactions.
Waypoint Icon
The waypoint icon is used when someone checks into a goal.
Cheer Icon
The cheer icon is used when one or many people cheered your goal.
Nudge Icon
The nudge icon is used when one or many people nudged your goal.
Goal Icon
The goal icon is used when a goal is created, completed, and archived. It is also used when a parent goal is added or edited after creation e.g. due date edits.
Edit Icon
The edit icon is used when a contributor was added/invited, parent goal deleted your goal as a milestone or deleted your metric, goal title edited, milestone is added/deleted/renamed, due date updated, target/starting value updated, and milestone reassigned.
Chat Icon
The chat icon is used when someone comments on a goal.
